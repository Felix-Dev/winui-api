Occurs when a call to [ScrollBy](/uwp/api/microsoft.ui.xaml.controls.scrollviewer.scrollby) or [ScrollTo](/uwp/api/microsoft.ui.xaml.controls.scrollviewer.scrollto) causes an animation to begin.
