## -description

Occurs when a call to [ZoomBy](/uwp/api/microsoft.ui.xaml.controls.primitives.scroller.zoomby) or [ZoomTo](/uwp/api/microsoft.ui.xaml.controls.primitives.scroller.zoomto) causes an animation to begin.
