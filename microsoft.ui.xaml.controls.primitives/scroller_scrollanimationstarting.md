## -description

Occurs when a call to [ScrollBy](/uwp/api/microsoft.ui.xaml.controls.primitives.scroller.scrollby) or [ScrollTo](/uwp/api/microsoft.ui.xaml.controls.primitives.scroller.scrollto) causes an animation to begin.
